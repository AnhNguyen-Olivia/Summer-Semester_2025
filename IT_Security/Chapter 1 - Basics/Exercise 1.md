Q: 
- Compare white hat vs black hat 
- Explain the details of secret services, companies, whistleblowers.
- What is shadow economy? and what is black market?
A: 
White hat is the person who usually a penetrate tester. Find exploitation and announce to the organization about the vulnerability. Black hat on the other hand find the exploitation and use it to their advantage in the cybercrime.

Secret service is usually a government organization or a secret one that is hidden from normal civilians. Their main adjective is national security via spying, sabotage or surveillance

companies use industrial espionage or sabotage for competitors. Also cooperating with secret service

Whistleblower is those believe to public secret information of wrongdoings.

shadow economy include attack and stole bitcoin and/or mining bitcoin without permission of owner’s machine. Phishing attacks stole information in area of online baking and accessing credit card information for fraudulent transactions.

Black market is a market for selling tools, information, vulnerability or hire black hackers for software vulnerabilities of IT systems
